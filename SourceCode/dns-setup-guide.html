Add DMARC Record (IMPORTANT)

This tells email providers how to handle emails that fail authentication:

TypeNameValueTTL
TXT _dmarc v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; rua=mailto:support@arinasystems.com; pct=100 3600
What this means:
  • p=quarantine - Put suspicious emails in spam folder
  • rua=mailto:support@arinasystems.com - Send reports to your email
  • pct=100 - Apply policy to 100% of emails
